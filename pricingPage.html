Frequently Asked
Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? We have compiled a list of the most common questions our customers ask about our home services, booking process, and safety protocols.

How do I book a service with Fixora?

Booking is simple! Just select your required service (Plumbing, Electrical, etc.), choose a convenient time slot, and provide your address. Our system will automatically assign the nearest verified expert to your location.

Are your technicians background-verified?

Yes, safety is our top priority. Every professional on our platform undergoes a rigorous background check, criminal record verification, and a technical skill test before they can join the Fixora team.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We offer a 30-day service guarantee. If the issue persists or the work isn't up to mark, we will send another expert to fix it for free, or offer you a partial refund depending on the situation.

Do I need to provide tools or spare parts?

Our experts bring all the necessary professional tools. For spare parts (like a new tap or wire), you can either buy them yourself or ask our technician to purchase them for you at an additional cost with a valid bill.

Is there an emergency service available at night?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 emergency support for critical issues like major water leaks or complete power failures. Just select the 'Emergency' tag while booking.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Absolutely. You can reschedule or cancel your booking directly from your dashboard or by contacting our support team, subject to our cancellation policy.

What areas does Fixora serve?

Fixora currently operates in selected cities and areas. Availability depends on your location and the service requested. Enter your location to check service coverage.
